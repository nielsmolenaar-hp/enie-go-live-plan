en-IE Go-Live — Technical Plan

What we need in place so Irish customers find us, click us, and we keep our rankings. Split into V1 (for the 3 July launch) and V2 (after launch). Tap Technical details under any item for the engineering specifics.

Tags: ✅ already live on staging · 🔧 still to build.

V1 — for the launch

Found by Google

Google can read every page on load · ✅ So pages can be indexed and rank — earning organic traffic. A page Google can't read earns nothing.

Technical detailsAll SEO content is server-side rendered; a build check blocks any page missing content. No indexable content loaded only by JavaScript.

Menus, configurator and prices are visible to Google · ✅ Our core commercial content is readable, not hidden behind a click.

Technical detailsNav links are real anchors; the configurator renders all options on load; live price + delivery are server-rendered with a last-known-good fallback.

Each page is its own "original" · ✅ (build-check 🔧) Stops Google treating tracking/filter versions as duplicates, which would split our ranking.

Technical detailsSelf-referencing canonical, normalised (https, no-www, lowercase, no tracking params). A build check confirms each canonical loads (200) and is in the sitemap.

We choose what Google indexes — nothing by accident · 🔧 We index pages that earn traffic and hide thin/duplicate ones; per language.

Technical detailsPer-locale index setting per page; default is hidden (noindex,follow) until reviewed; key commercial pages stay indexed.

Clean, stable URLs · ✅ Keeps the ranking value our current URLs already hold.

Technical detailsFlat lowercase URLs with the country/language prefix; existing addresses reused; any change redirected.

Old URLs redirect, and no link is a dead end · 🔧 Carries ranking value across the migration and never strands a customer or Google.

Technical detailsSame-moment 301s (list already defined); removed/discontinued products redirect to the nearest page, never 404; every internal link resolves to a live page; AI bots get the same pages, not a geo-redirect.

Sitemap + robots.txt · ✅ Google discovers all ~6,754 pages fast, and all search + AI bots are welcome.

Technical detailsMulti-tier sitemap (live on staging) + correct robots.txt allowing all bots. Outstanding: daily refresh + health-check crawl.

One clean headline, no leftover placeholders, consistent locale codes · 🔧 Avoids missing/duplicate headlines and {{placeholder}} text leaking to customers or Google.

Technical detailsBuild check: exactly one H1 per page; template tokens validated as resolved; locale codes lowercase everywhere with uppercase variants redirected; no two pages can publish to the same URL.

Fast & mobile-ready

Product pages pass Google's speed score · ✅ Fast pages rank better and convert better; a slow page can't ship.

Technical detailsDeploy-blocking budgets on product pages (LCP ≤2.0s, CLS ≤0.05, INP ≤150ms, JS ≤250KB). Category pages monitored.

Main image loads first, nothing jumps, light first load · ✅ The page feels instant and stable, lifting conversion.

Technical detailsHero image eager + preloaded, rest lazy; reserved space prevents layout shift; only first-screen JavaScript loads up front.

Mobile essentials · ✅ Protects mobile rankings (where most Irish traffic is) and looks right on a phone.

Technical detailsViewport tag, one responsive page (no separate mobile site / AMP), and share-preview/head tags (charset, language, Open Graph) — all live.

Trust & rich results

Product pages show price, stock & breadcrumb in Google · ✅ Rich results lift click-through against competitors in the Irish results.

Technical detailsProduct + Offer + Breadcrumb structured data, build-checked. No product star rating at launch (company rating only).

Category pages show their product list & breadcrumb · 🔧 Helps category terms rank — the biggest Irish demand pool.

Technical detailsCategory pages send a product-list + breadcrumb (modern schema). Currently still on the old format on staging — needs the rewrite.

Company rating shows in Google and on the page instantly · ✅ Trust for a brand new to Irish customers; no blank widget at the deciding moment.

Technical detailsCompany-level Trustpilot rating in structured data + server-rendered on first paint; hides cleanly if unavailable.

V2 — after launch

Full mobile optimisation Best-in-class mobile beyond the essentials.

Technical detailsFirst-screen fit on small phones, one lighter image per product on mobile, larger tap targets + 16px inputs, respect "reduce motion".

Product- and category-specific Trustpilot ratings Star ratings for the exact product/category, not just the company — more on-page social proof.

Technical detailsNeeds product-specific review data + the reviews block; ride the new category schema.

Earned filter pages (filter listings) Capture real filter demand (e.g. "aluminium water bottles") without diluting the main pages.

Technical detailsServer-rendered filters, crawlable pagination, edge caching. Currently out of scope.

360° spins, video & video rich results Richer media on hero products lifts confidence to buy.

Caching at scale + remaining company pages Fast pages at full catalogue scale; proper homes for /our-promises, contact, etc.
